Inventions refer to different sins and new ways of committing them. Inventions refer to humanities new ways of sinning against God and others, it is evading the truth, easing our own conscience, and convincing ourselves that what was done was not really sinful or that bad. It is when a believer plans a sin and then asks for forgiveness afterwards knowing full well that it was in the heart all along to do the wrong thing from the beginning. It looks like David with Bathsheeba and then moving on to Uriah to cover the first mistake. When we do that God still forgives us and extends His mercy towards us but we greive Him in the process and without a repentant heart there is no force to stop inventions from being made and implemented further. It turns into a pattern: fall into sin, reap the consequences, cry out to God for forgiveness and then repeat the process all over again. It starts in the heart and without eyes to see it for what it is we become powerless to stop the course of action. These types of inventions are totally different than the inventions of (Proverbs 8:12) I wisdom dwell with prudence, and find out knowledge of witty inventions. This verse is in reference to wisdom to create things beneficial for self and all of humanity.
